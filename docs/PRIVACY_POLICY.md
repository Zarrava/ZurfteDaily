**Last updated:** May 28, 2026  
**App name:** Zurfte Daily

## Summary

Zurfte Daily is a motivational quotes app. We designed it to respect your privacy. **We do not collect, sell, or share your personal information.** Data stays on your device unless you choose to share a quote using your phone’s share feature.

## Information we do not collect

We do **not** ask for:

- Your name, email, or phone number  
- Location data  
- Contacts or photos  
- Account registration  
- Analytics or advertising IDs tied to you (this app does not include third-party ad SDKs)

## Information stored on your device

The app uses **local storage only** (AsyncStorage on your phone) to save:

1. **Favorite quotes** you save manually  
2. **Dark mode preference** (on or off)

This data:

- Stays on your device  
- Is not sent to our servers (we do not operate backend servers for this app)  
- Can be removed by clearing app data in Android Settings or uninstalling the app  

## Share functionality

When you tap **Share Quote**, the app opens your device’s standard share menu (SMS, social apps, email, etc.). **We do not receive the content you share.** Sharing is handled entirely by your device and the app you choose.

## Children's privacy

Zurfte Daily is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Security

Because data is stored locally, protecting your device (screen lock, OS updates) helps keep your favorites private.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when the app changes. The “Last updated” date will change accordingly. Continued use of the app after updates means you accept the revised policy.

## Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access or delete personal data. Because we do not collect personal data on our servers, deleting app data or uninstalling the app removes locally stored favorites and settings.
